# Noteworthy — Tutoring / Music Lessons

## Identity

**Business name:** Noteworthy Studio  
**Industry:** K–12 tutoring + private music instruction  
**Brand personality:** Warm, scholarly, stage-ready. Equal respect for GPAs and recitals.  
**Primary conversion:** Book a trial lesson  
**Trust mechanism:** Credentials, retention stats, parent voice, clear policies  
**Emotional tone:** Encouraging, structured, never condescending  
**Image style:** Studio, sheet music, students mid-lesson  
**Sitemap:** Home, Lessons (subjects + studio week), Pricing, About, Contact  

---

## Industry translation

- **Dual product** — academics and music need separate clarity; homepage introduces both; Lessons page splits card-grid (academics) vs icon grid (music).
- **Parent buyer** — testimonial-hero and FAQs address scheduling, makeup policy, and online vs in-person.
- **Rhythm & milestones** — steps widget for onboarding; schedule-table for weekly studio availability.

---

## preset.json

- **Palette:** **Cool slate** surfaces (`#f8fafc`, `#f1f5f9`) — no warm beige. **Indigo** brand (`#4f46e5` on light, `#1e1b4b` / `#0f172a` dark bands) with **cyan** CTAs on dark (`#22d3ee`, text `#0f172a`). Reads crisp and studio-lit, not parchment.
- **Typography:** **Fraunces** 600 + **Source Sans 3** 400 — editorial serif + readable UI sans.
- **Style:** `rounded`, `pill` buttons — family-friendly.

---

## Differentiation

- Opener: **banner** hero with education/music eyebrow (not split-hero / not auto-trades).
- **Image-tabs** for “how we work” story beats.
- **Schedule-table** + **accordion** on Lessons with sidebar info (not only on Contact).
- Underused widgets: **testimonial-hero**, **image-tabs**, **schedule-table**, **steps**, **rich-text** (pricing add-on).
