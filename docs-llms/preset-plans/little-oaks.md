# Little Oaks — Daycare / Preschool

## Identity

**Business name:** Little Oaks Early Learning  
**Industry:** Licensed day care / preschool (ages 6 weeks–5)  
**Brand personality:** Nurturing, transparent, outdoors-minded — parents should feel safety and pedagogy first.  
**Primary conversion:** Schedule a tour / waitlist  
**Trust:** Licensing, ratios, secure entry, parent communication  
**Palette:** Cool **slate** (`#f8fafc`) + **mint** secondary (`#ecfdf5`) — no warm beige. **Forest green** (`#14532d` / `#166534`) highlights with **gold** CTAs on dark (`#fbbf24`).
