# Tailside - Preset Plan

Tailside is a polished small-business website for a neighborhood veterinary clinic. The site should feel familiar, reassuring, and easy to act on: clear service categories, friendly team credibility, practical new-patient guidance, and a low-friction contact path.

## Phase 0 - Industry Strategy Brief

- **Business archetype:** Appointment-based local healthcare service for dogs and cats.
- **Primary conversion:** Book a visit or call the clinic for a care question.
- **Trust mechanism:** Licensed veterinary expertise, clean practical communication, visible team bios, visit-process clarity, and clear urgent-care guidance.
- **Decision mode:** Considered and emotional. Pet owners want competence, warmth, and a clinic that explains what happens next.
- **Brand personality:** Calm, capable, friendly, and lightly clinical without feeling sterile or corporate.
- **Content posture:** Service-led and trust-led, supported by warm clinic imagery and plain-language explanations.
- **Audience model:** Pet owners with dogs and cats, including new puppy/kitten owners, adult-pet households, senior-pet caregivers, and people with same-week sick-care concerns.
- **Required page jobs:** Home for first impression and booking path; Services for care categories; New Patients for prep and first-visit questions; About for team and clinic values; Contact for hours, location, phone, and urgent guidance.
- **No-go patterns:** Cartoon pet branding, gimmicky paw-print overload, sterile hospital minimalism, emergency-room drama, vague "we do everything" copy, and hard-sell CTA stacking.
- **Opener candidates:** Warm clinic welcome with image beside copy, service-first page intro, new-patient handoff, and team-led trust opener.
- **Closing pattern:** Calm appointment nudge with phone and booking options, not pressure.

## Phase 1 - Plan

### Identity

- **Name:** Tailside
- **Industry:** Veterinarian / small animal clinic
- **Tagline seed:** "Veterinary care for the pets at your side."
- **Site shape:** A typical five-page small-business site: Home, Services, New Patients, About, Contact.

### Visual Direction

- **Palette:** Warm clinic neutrals with soft sage surfaces and a deep green-teal highlight palette. The accent is medical enough to feel trustworthy, but softened so the site still feels like a neighborhood clinic.
- **Typography:** `Literata` headings at weight `600` for warm authority, paired with `Public Sans` body text at weight `400` for clarity.
- **Style:** Slightly rounded corners, default spacing, auto button shape.

### Sitemap Rationale

- **Home:** Establish trust, services, visit flow, and booking.
- **Services:** Make care categories easy to scan and answer common scope questions.
- **New Patients:** Explain what to bring, what happens at the first visit, and where forms belong.
- **About:** Introduce the clinic promise and team.
- **Contact:** Put phone, hours, location, and urgent guidance in one clear place.

### Header

- Logo text: `Tailside`
- Header contact: `(555) 555-0100`, `Tue-Sat appointments`
- CTA: `Book a visit` linking to `contact.html`
- Sticky header, standard-primary surface, no transparent hero overlay.

### Footer

- Highlight-primary footer with clinic summary, hours, urgent-care guidance, and quick links.

### Page Strategy

- Use `split-hero` as a familiar small-business opener on content pages.
- Use `icon-card-grid` for services because veterinary visitors scan by care need.
- Use `timeline` on Home and New Patients to make the visit flow concrete.
- Use `profile-grid` on About to make care feel human.
- Use `accordion` with sidebar info for practical questions.
- Use `contact-details` and `map` on Contact for the expected small-business contact experience.
- Use `action-bar` as the consistent closing conversion pattern.

### Widget Usage Summary

- `split-hero`: page openers with editable image placeholders.
- `trust-bar`: quick credibility strip under the homepage hero.
- `icon-card-grid`: services and value summaries.
- `features-split`: clinic values and life-stage care.
- `timeline`: visit process.
- `resource-list`: new-patient forms and documents.
- `profile-grid`: clinic team.
- `accordion`: FAQs with contact/sidebar support.
- `contact-details`: primary contact surface.
- `map`: location and visit logistics.
- `action-bar`: soft CTA close.

### Menus

- Main menu: Home, Services, New Patients, About, Contact.
- Footer menu: Home, Services, New Patients, About, Contact.

### Differentiation Notes

Tailside is intentionally not an experimental preset. It should read like a strong default website a local veterinary clinic could launch quickly, with familiar navigation, clear service categories, warm staff trust, and direct booking/contact routes.
