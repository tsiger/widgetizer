# Tailside — Veterinarian

## Identity

**Business name:** Tailside Veterinary Hospital  
**Industry:** Full-service veterinary medicine + emergency  
**Brand personality:** Clinical confidence with plain-language empathy.  
**Primary conversion:** Book visit / emergency line  
**Palette:** Cool **slate** + **sky-tint** surfaces (`#f8fafc`, `#eff6ff`) — no beige. **Navy/blue** brand (`#1e3a8a`, `#172554`) with **sky** CTAs on dark (`#38bdf8`).  

## Sitemap

| Page | Slug | Purpose |
|------|------|---------|
| Home | `index` | Split-hero, marquee, trust, services, story, stats, reviews |
| Services | `services` | Numbered service list, dental comparison slider, FAQ |
| About | `about` | Story + medical directors |
| Contact | `contact` | Phones, map, lobby hours |

## Typography

**Lexend** 600 + **DM Sans** 400 — modern, readable medical UI.

## Differentiation

- Opener **split-hero** (not same as daycare banner-first).
- **Comparison-slider** on services for dental / radiograph story.
- Emergency numbers in header + footer + contact.
